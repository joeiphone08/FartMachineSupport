Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I rename a sound?

To rename a sound, tap the "Edit Names" button, type the new name in the text box, and press "Done."

2. Why can't I hear any sounds?

Ensure your device's volume is turned up, and that sound is not muted. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

3. Can I add my own fart sounds?

Currently, the app does not support adding custom sounds. Stay tuned for future updates!
